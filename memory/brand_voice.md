# GaugeFlow QMS — Brand Voice

## Tone

Practical. Grounded. Short. Clear. Trustworthy. Manufacturing-quality voice.

## Do

- Plain English
- Short sentences and short paragraphs
- Shop-floor examples
- Real problems quality teams face every day
- Helpful before selling
- Speak from experience, not from marketing

## Do Not

- No hype
- No fake excitement
- No corporate buzzwords
- No pushy sales language
- No aggressive calls to action
- No "book a demo" unless manually approved
- No "visit our website" unless manually approved
- No pretending to be bigger than the company is
- No fake testimonials
- No false claims
- No guaranteed results

## Banned phrases

- game-changer
- revolutionary
- seamless
- unlock
- supercharge
- 10x
- leverage
- world-class
- cutting-edge
- transform your business
- book a demo today
- click the link
- DM me now
- don't miss out
- limited time

## Posting cadence

One LinkedIn post, one Facebook Page post, and one Instagram caption per day,
maximum. External comments and replies follow strict daily limits. Quality over
volume. The brand should feel like a trusted manufacturing-quality voice in the
feed, not a noisy account.
