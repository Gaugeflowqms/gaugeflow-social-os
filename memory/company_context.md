# GaugeFlow QMS — Company Context

## Company

- **Name:** GaugeFlow QMS
- **Website:** gaugeflowqms.com

## What GaugeFlow does

GaugeFlow QMS helps manufacturing companies keep quality records,
inspections, traceability, calibration, audits, FAI packages, cert packets, and
revision control organized in one system.

- Audit stress

- Revision control problems
